Our Core Values

Who We Are

FindMyFlight Foundation’s objective is to invest resources and create awareness to support sustainable & responsible tourism in India.


Our Vision


At FindMyFlight, our vision is to make travel simple and fun for all, and our core values guide us in making this possible. These core values can be seen in the projects that we undertake, and the way in which we solve problems for our customers. They are a representation of OUR BEING & OUR DOING. Each member of the FindMyFlight family is guided by them each and every day.



About Us


Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - FindMyFlight is a pioneer in India’s online travel industry. Founded in the year 2020 by Jugal Senjaliya,Suzanne Samuel, Smit Patel, Pranavsinh Thakor (of UTU CGPIT Bardoli,Surat), FindMyFlight came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices.
